What is a Virtual Assistant?

A virtual assistant is a person who works from a distant location to provide administrative services to clients. Virtual assistants are typically self-employed contract employees, but you can also work with a virtual assistant agency, just like Click N Talk.


One of the primary benefits of hiring a virtual assistant is that small businesses can pick and choose which services they require. This level of adaptability can save business owners thousands of dollars each year while also allowing them to expand their contracts as their company grows and requires more services.

What makes a good Virtual Assistant?

Great job candidates have phenomenal verbal and written communication skills, as well as open and forthcoming communication practices, and are viewed as a trusted, achievable partners by their clients. Time management and organizational skills, as well as the natural ability to take control of a situation and arrange it for the benefit of a client, are required.


It is also necessary to have prior experience with or knowledge of cloud-based communications technology such as file sharing, password managers, and phone and video conferencing.


Lastly, individuals who employ virtual assistants want to reduce their stress and disorientation. If you notice a client is having issues, offer to assist them with something you aren't currently doing. This increases your workload and makes them even more reliant on you.
