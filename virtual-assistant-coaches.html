How Click N Talk Virtual Assistants help Life and Business Coaches?

So, what distinguishes a virtual assistant for business and life coaches from other types of virtual assistants?


It all boils down to the type of work you do. As a coach, you face unique challenges that a simple masonry business may not have.

Managing an inbox and a calendar

Personal and business coaching are difficult because they necessitate the consistent managing of a calendar and a diverse range of clients, each with their own set of needs. How do you remain in control of everything while also giving your full attention to the unique needs of each client? Hire a Click N Talk virtual assistant who works with coaches. Our agents are skilled at managing your schedule, with your calendar software—to avoid overlaps.

Promoting your company

When you're centered on your clients, how will you ever have time to advertise your business? How can you step back from the daily needs of your company to manage the logistics of constructing these promotional tools, for example, if you want to create a webinar? You hire a virtual assistant who has worked with them before.

Handling the business aspects of your company so you can focus entirely on your clients

You almost certainly became a coach because you wanted to work with individuals personally. You wanted to assist them. That can only happen if you give them the one-on-one time they need. If you're constantly occupied with data entry, bookkeeping, and communication, you won't be able to run the business you want to run. A virtual assistant can fill in the gaps so you can focus on your work.

Tasks that coaches can delegate to a virtual assistant

  • Scheduling follow-ups
  • Manage the marketing of your business
  • Bookkeeping and tracking of your expenses
  • Arrange and plan for your travels
  • General tasks like responding to reviews online
