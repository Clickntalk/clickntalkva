Hire The Best Virtual Sales Team for your business with Click N Talk!

A virtual sales team is a group of sales representatives who work entirely from their homes. This means that there is no shared office room; instead, each sales representative works from their preferred location. A virtual sales team differs from a hybrid team, in which each employee chooses whether to work remotely or in person at a physical office location. In-person meetings are not an option for virtual sales teams.

Virtual Sales Team may do these tasks for you

  • Making appointments
  • CRM activity updates
  • Entering data
  • Telephone cold calling
  • Promotion and growth of sales
  • Generating leads
  • Prospective analysis
  • Taking phone calls

Advantages of Hiring Virtual Sales Team


Minimal Training

The benefit of hiring independent contractors is that they require little to no training. Simply outline the job description and find virtual sales assistants with the necessary skills. This is a huge benefit for busy sales leaders who require extra sales service at the end of a quarter but do not have the time to train a new employee.

Flexibility

Virtual sales assistants can be hired on a contract, monthly, or per-project basis. The transition to virtual sales people provides the organization with greater flexibility to manage busy seasons (such as end-of-quarter quota sprints) as well as changing customer and business market needs. Virtual sales teams provide businesses with the flexibility they need to measure their efforts up and down to meet changing needs and priorities.

Reduce Overhead

Virtual sales representatives do not require an actual office, equipment, or extensive training resources. Furthermore, if you only hire contractors, you avoid having to pay for employee benefits and insurance. As your sales organization grows, you will have more money to invest in resources and support or even additional virtual sales assistants.

Increase Productivity

Assume you would like to invest in virtual workers rather than contract employees. The good news is that remote employees are more efficient and less likely to leave their jobs. Hiring virtual sales employees can thus be a profitable investment even with some supplemental overhead costs for benefits.
