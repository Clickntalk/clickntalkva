Three Basic Essentials of Handling Your Schedule

Prioritization

As your company grows, you must decide which activities are and are not worth your time. This means that previously worth having activities may no longer be feasible in your packed schedule.

Automation

Is there anything in your schedule that can be replaced by automation or delegated? If this is the case, it will free up more time for your high-priority work. You should also consider your potential to automate your scheduling processes by utilizing available tools and software.

Organization

A good schedule is an efficient tool for day planning. It should not be a scattershot to-do list, but rather a list of real priorities that make some sense given the time you have available.
