Higher Success Rates with your leads with Click N Talk’s Outbound Calling Services!

Outbound calling is the process of contacting prospective and current customers in order to pique their interest in the company's services and products. It is the most effective way of communicating with leads, as well as the quickest and most precise method of receiving feedback.
An outbound call's primary goal is to contact frequent or potential customers in order to achieve business goals like promoting a brand, scheduling appointments, and lead generation.

Warm or Cold Calling? Which one do you prefer? Click N Talk Outbound Calling Services offers you both!

There are two categories of outbound calls, mainly warm calling and cold calling. Warm calling is the result of previous interaction, referral, or maybe a cold call. These leads have already expressed an interest in your services.

On the other hand, Cold calling is reaching a potential customer who has never interacted with your company and has not expressed preceding interest in your products or services.

What type of calls Click N Talk Agents can handle?

Telemarketing

Also known as telephone marketing, it is one method of contacting, canvassing and qualifying a prospective customer. Click N Talk agents can make outbound calls to prospects to inform them about your company's services, explain the benefits, and persuade them to use or purchase the products. Our callers evaluate the customer's needs, gauge their interest, establish rapport, schedule appointments, and keep striving to convert this prospect into a qualified lead.

Lead Generation

Attracting people’s interest is what Click N Talk agents do to generate and pre-qualify your leads. Through cold calling prospects, We pique the interest of leads enough for them to request more information from you. We can also use this method to separate the strong leads from the bad ones, allowing our agents to focus on who to contact and nurture.

Follow - Up

Click N Talk agents can also send direct mail to prospects as a means of capturing their attention. We can send marketing emails such as newsletters, brochures, catalogs, and sales letters directly to the customer's inbox. Once the client has accepted and opened the emails, Click N Talk agents can begin calling and following up on these leads.

Appointment Setting

Click N Talk agents are excellent appointment setters. We can arrange for a meeting between a sales representative and a prospective client, remind them of upcoming appointments, and verify the best time and date to communicate. Click N Talk agents are efficient, precise, and engaging which keeps the prospects remain connected.
