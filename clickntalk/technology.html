What are the main types of marketing technology?

Advertising

Advertising technology allows you to reach audiences that would otherwise be inaccessible. This could be done through Search engine ads, Facebook remarketing ads, or targeting a specific work title on LinkedIn.


Analytics

Your marketing technology stack should be built around analytics. Analytics can be a separate product or a feature of another tool, for example, Bynder offers content and ROI analytics within your Digital Asset Management tool.

Content Management

Marketers create content in order to promote their products or service. However, if the organization fails to quickly locate and disseminate marketing materials, those assets (and any opportunities they may have created) are lost. That is why an efficient digital asset management system is critical for maximizing the value of your branded content.


CRM

Implementing an efficient CRM system is crucial to market performance. CRM helps you generate more worth from your existing base of clients by facilitating customer retention, which increases the ROI of your customer acquisition efforts.


Social Media

Social media transactions can be maximized in a variety of ways by utilizing technology. For example, if you manage multiple Twitter accounts, TweetDeck consolidates all of your Twitter accounts into a single, convenient dashboard.
