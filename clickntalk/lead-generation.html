Lead Generation

Have you heard the term “lead generation” all the time? It is the process of gaining the interest of potential customers in order to increase sales in the future. A lead is someone who has expressed an interest in your products or services but is not yet qualified to receive them. They can be generated from different sources such as Facebook advertisements, referrals, former clients, Google resources, LinkedIn, and other sources.

How important is lead generation to your business?

Target the right prospects

You target specific customers who are more likely to purchase your product or service. This saves time and money while optimizing your business's processes and improving sales.


Build brand awareness

Informing your leads about your business and its product is always a part of lead generation, whether they learn about you on their own or you reach out to them with info about your product's features. Leads can then spread publicity about your brand, bringing you even more customers.

How do Click N Talk Agents generate leads?

Content Marketing

Develop and deliver content with an emphasis on education rather than selling. Content can be created in a variety of formats, including blogs, infographics, videos, case studies, and white papers.


Social Media

Depending on your industry, each social networking site can be a valuable lead generation platform. B2C requires Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and Snapchat, while B2B involves LinkedIn.

Email Marketing

You can start a one-on-one communication with your recipient by sharing a blog, inviting them to a webinar, informing them about a product update, or offering a discount via email.

Webinars

Webinars require participants to provide their email addresses, making them an ideal lead generation activity. Timely webinars with relevant issues and quality speakers can help you create a brand for your company.

Pay-per-click Advertisement (PPC)

When a visitor clicks on a PPC ad, they are directed to a landing page that collects the lead's information. PPC ads work but they consist of keywords relevant to your audience and appear on Google's first page.

Display Advertisement

Display ads, also known as banner ads, are digital versions of print advertisements. Display ads can benefit from clear copy, a compelling CTA (call-to-action), illustrations, and animation.
