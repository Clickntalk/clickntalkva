What is lead nurturing?

Developing and strengthening relationships with buyers at each stage of the sales funnel is known as lead nurturing. A successful lead nurturing program concentrates communication and marketing efforts on listening to prospects' demands and offering them the information and answering their inquiries. Lead nurturing is important to build trust, increase brand awareness, and maintain a strong connection with them until they are ready to purchase.
